A little About Us.

Shoe Jack City is a first of its kind mobile gaming resell app that allows players to
buy, sell and compete for rare, high-end sneakers in a battle royale tournament.
Inspired by the MegaMan Battle Network Series, players can choose thier Anomaly,
collect battle drives to unleash powerful attacks and hidden abilities.

Buy and/or Sell sneakers, and Dominate your way through intense challenges and
tournaments. Play against friends and compete in 1v1 tournament battle royale to
win top tier sneakers and reduce your sellers transaction fee!
